ledro keeps your money private. Everything you enter stays on your device. There is no account, no bank connection, no analytics, and no ads. We never see, collect, transmit, or sell your data, and we run no servers that hold your information.

What ledro handles

The only information in ledro is what you type in — the names and amounts of your stacks, budgets, and line items. It is stored on your device and is never sent to us or anyone else. ledro does not collect:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone) — there is no sign-up.
  • Bank or financial-account credentials — ledro never connects to a bank.
  • Usage analytics, device identifiers, advertising IDs, or location.
  • Anything through third-party trackers or ad SDKs — ledro has none.

Where your data lives

Your data is saved only in ledro on your device. If you use device backups (such as Apple's iCloud device backup), it is included there — controlled by you and Apple, not by us. Deleting the app removes the data, and we keep no copy because we never received one.

Sharing

We don't share or sell your information, because we don't have it. ledro makes no network requests that send your financial data anywhere.

If you contact us

If you email us, we receive your email address and whatever you write, and we use it only to reply to you.

Children's privacy

ledro is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country), and we do not knowingly collect information from them. Since the app collects no personal information at all, this is largely moot.

Your rights

Because your data lives only on your device, you control it entirely: edit or delete any entry, reset everything from Settings, or delete the app to erase all of it. Laws such as GDPR and CCPA cover personal data that companies hold about you — ledro holds none, so there is nothing for us to access, export, or delete on your behalf.

Changes

If we change how ledro handles data, we will update this policy and revise the date above.
